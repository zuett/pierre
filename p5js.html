The MetaPortal

A portal through art

Concept

For this project, I drew inspiration to craft a series of diverse visual illustrations, utilizing P5.js as the optimal platform. Delving into the capabilities of sine, cosine, and tangent functions, complemented by the frameCount function, I embarked on exploring various patterns to create an immersive visual journey. With each creation, I uncovered new techniques, enriching my understanding of the language. Having completed a total of 8 distinct pieces, I turned my attention to crafting a final home page to showcase these visual compositions. Initially envisioning a dynamic layout resembling a vast 3D globe adorned with smaller globes, each representing an individual artwork, I sought to enhance user engagement. However, I found the initial design lacked the desired appeal. Revisiting the concept, I aimed to cultivate a sense of curiosity by concealing the final visual effects behind question marks. Each question mark serves as a gateway to a different piece, deliberately leaving users to explore and immerse themselves in the experience without predetermined guidance. To further enhance the ambiance, I integrated a diverse range of audio elements into each piece, ranging from Lo-Fi beats to carefully selected songs, tailored to accentuate the desired vibe within the visual design portal.
