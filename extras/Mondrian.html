MONDRIAN'S MIRAGE

A portal through art

Mondrian's Mirage is an immersive XR project that transforms Piet Mondrian's 1922 painting Composition with Blue, Red, Yellow, and Black into an interactive 3D environment using Unity 3D. I translated Mondrian’s abstract forms into architectural spaces, allowing users to navigate through his iconic lines and colors. The experience culminates in a dynamic sequence where users are teleported into the painting’s structure, simulating a freefall through its geometric forms. By combining 3D modeling, virtual navigation, and immersive transitions, this project explores the boundary between physical and abstract art, offering a unique, interactive reinterpretation of Mondrian’s work.
