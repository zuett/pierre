Whispers from the depth

A Live XR performance performed at the black box at NYUAD

Whispers from the Depth is an immersive XR performance that leverages advanced motion capture technology, real-time animation, and immersive sound design to address the critical issue of marine pollution. Utilizing a Rokoko motion-capture suit, I directed the seamless translation of the performer’s movements into a dynamic virtual avatar, projected in real-time onto a large-scale canvas. This integration of live performance with digital environments created a compelling, interactive narrative that deepened audience engagement.

I developed and curated the visual framework, featuring an ocean goddess surrounded by meticulously animated marine life—whales, dolphins, and turtles—symbolizing the devastating effects of human-induced ocean pollution. The audio design was crafted to complement the visual narrative, incorporating ambient soundscapes and authentic marine acoustics with an escalating musical score to heighten emotional impact and convey the urgency of environmental action.

By combining XR technologies, motion capture, and immersive audiovisual storytelling, Whispers from the Depth exemplifies the use of cutting-edge techniques to merge art and environmental advocacy, fostering a powerful call to action for ocean conservation.
