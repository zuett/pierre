03 : About me

Greetings! I'm a Junior at NYUAD, majoring in Interactive Media with a minor in Computer Science, and pursuing a double major in Film and New Media. My academic and creative journey revolves around blending technology and storytelling to craft innovative experiences.

Through diverse projects, I've gained hands-on expertise in immersive technologies, game development, and interactive media design. My work spans creating VR experiences with Unity and Cinemachine, developing XR performances, and designing interactive portals with p5.js. These projects have sharpened my skills in creative coding, dynamic visual storytelling, and user-centered design.

I have a strong foundation in hardware prototyping with Arduino, showcased in music-based games, and an eye for designing intuitive user experiences, as seen in data-driven visualizations. My ability to merge cinematic storytelling with interactive mediums is evident in projects like "Memory in Frames" and "Fever-Dream," where I explored the synergy of film and VR.

My technical toolkit includes Unity, Python, C++, p5.js, Arduino, and JavaScript, with a focus on using these tools to create immersive and meaningful narratives. From VR games to branching storylines, I consistently challenge myself to push the boundaries of storytelling and innovation. Beyond technology, I value collaboration, community, and creativity. As I continue to expand my skills and explore new possibilities, I aim to create experiences that not only captivate but also inspire and connect people.
