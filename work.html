Whispers from the depth

A Live XR performance performed at the black box at NYUAD

"Whispers from the Depth" is an immersive XR performance that serves as a poignant artistic testament to the plight of marine life affected by the staggering volumes of plastic and debris inundating our oceans each year. This multidimensional experience commences with a 30-second visual narrative, revealing the heart-wrenching struggle of marine creatures battling the consequences of human negligence. A haunting symphony of a majestic whale's cry pierces the silence, echoing the profound anguish inflicted upon these vulnerable beings by our actions. At the heart of this performance stands a performer, adorned in a resplendent rokoko motion-capture suit, a conduit between the corporeal and the virtual. Every movement she enacts is translated in real-time, giving life to an avatar projected onto a sprawling canvas behind her. The scene unfolds gradually, unveiling a goddess figure swathed in hues of cerulean, an embodiment of the ocean and its inhabitants. Poised atop an expansive expanse of water, she performs an evocative tribute, a dance revering the souls of those lost to the sea's merciless embrace. Behind her, an ethereal spectacle unfolds—a celestial whale, an emblem of oceanic supremacy, its form suspended in the heavens, a sentinel overseeing the departed. Among the spectral chorus are the spirits of dolphins, turtles traversing the horizon, and penguins gathered upon distant shores, emissaries for the myriad creatures felled by human indifference. The accompanying soundtrack mirrors the emotional crescendo, commencing with mournful whale cries interwoven with a tranquil melody that gradually intensifies. As the tempo quickens, an escalating rhythm heightens the palpable tension, crafting a crescendo that speaks to the urgency of the marine life's plight, demanding acknowledgement and action. Through a symphony of visuals, movement, and sound, "Whispers from the Depth" beckons viewers into a realm where art converges with advocacy, compelling introspection and fostering a collective responsibility to protect the fragile harmony of our oceans.
